Cookies
A cookie is a small text file that a website saves on your computer or mobile device
when you visit the site. In general, cookies have two main purposes: to improve your
browsing experience by remembering your actions and preferences and to help us
analyze our website traffic.
What to do with Cookies?
We use cookies to help us analyze traffic to the Website, to help us improve website
performance and usability, and to make the Website more secure. Third-party cookies
help us use Google Analytics to count, track, and analyze visits to the Website. This
helps us understand how people are using our websites and where we need to make
improvements. These third-party cookies do not specifically identify you.
